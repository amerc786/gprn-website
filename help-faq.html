Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using GPRN. Can't find what you need? Contact our support team below.

Getting Started

Click "Get Started" on the homepage and choose whether you are a Locum GP or a Practice Manager. Fill in your details, verify your email address, and complete your profile. Locum GPs will need to provide their GMC number and upload key documents. The process takes approximately 2-3 minutes.
GPRN is free for locum GPs to register and use. Practices pay a small service fee per confirmed booking. There are no hidden charges or subscription fees. Full pricing details are available during practice registration.
Practices post available shifts with details such as date, time, session type, and requirements. Locum GPs browse available shifts, filtered by their preferred health boards and availability. When a locum applies for a shift, the practice can review their profile and confirm the booking. Both parties receive notifications throughout the process.
GPRN covers all seven health boards in Wales: Aneurin Bevan, Betsi Cadwaladr, Cardiff and Vale, Cwm Taf Morgannwg, Hywel Dda, Powys, and Swansea Bay. We are focused exclusively on Welsh primary care.

For Locums

Once logged in, navigate to "Available Shifts" from your dashboard. You can filter shifts by health board, date range, session type (AM, PM, Full Day), and other criteria. You can also view shifts on your calendar for a visual overview of availability.
Yes. You can set your default rates for AM, PM, Full Day, and On-Call sessions from the "My Rates" page. You can also set practice-specific rates if you have different arrangements with particular practices. Your rates are shared with practices when you apply for a shift.
When you apply for a shift, your offer (including your rates and profile) is sent to the practice. The practice reviews all offers and can accept or decline. Once accepted, the shift is confirmed in your calendar. You can withdraw an offer before it is accepted if your plans change.
Your booking reliability score reflects how consistently you honour confirmed bookings. Cancellations and no-shows reduce your score. A score above 95% gives you priority access to shifts. Practices can see your reliability score when reviewing your offers, so maintaining a high score helps you secure more work.

For Practices

From your dashboard, click "Post a Shift". Fill in the date, session type, start and end times, number of patients, and any special requirements. You can mark shifts as urgent for priority visibility. Once posted, the shift becomes visible to all locum GPs registered for your health board area.
You can browse locum profiles from the "Find Locums" section or wait for locums to apply for your posted shifts. Each locum profile includes their qualifications, clinical system experience, booking reliability score, and rates. You can directly contact locums or accept their shift offers.
Currently, shifts are posted individually to ensure accurate details for each date. However, you can quickly duplicate a previous shift posting to save time. We are working on a bulk posting feature for future releases.
After a shift is completed, the agreed rate is processed through the platform. Practices receive a monthly invoice detailing all confirmed bookings. Payment terms and billing details can be managed from the "Billing" section of your dashboard. All rates are agreed upfront before booking confirmation.

Billing & Payments

Payment arrangements are agreed directly between the locum and the practice. GPRN facilitates the booking and provides transparent rate information, but does not process payments directly. Most practices pay locums within 30 days of the completed shift.
No. GPRN is transparent about all costs. Locums use the platform for free. Practices are charged a clearly stated service fee per booking, which is visible before confirming any shift. There are no sign-up fees, monthly subscriptions, or hidden charges.
GPRN displays whether you have opted into NHS Pension on your profile. Pension contributions remain between you, the practice, and the pension scheme. We recommend using tools like Locum Organiser to track your pension contributions across all your locum work.

Technical

GPRN works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser up to date for the best experience. Internet Explorer is not supported.
Yes. GPRN is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets. You can browse shifts, manage offers, and receive notifications from any device. A dedicated mobile app is planned for the future.
