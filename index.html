How GPRN Works

Three simple steps to connect locums with practices. No fuss, no delays.

1

Sign Up

Create your profile in minutes. Locums add availability, practices list open shifts.

2

Match

Our smart matching connects the right locum to the right shift based on location, skills, and availability.

3

Book & Go

Confirm shifts instantly. Manage bookings, rates, and schedules all from your dashboard.
